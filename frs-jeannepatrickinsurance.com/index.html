Products Offered


There are four strategic Medicare insurance coverage products. Each offers a unique set of benefits and restrictions.

  • PPOs
  • HMOs
  • Medicare Supplements
  • PDPs
PPO

Preferred provider organization or PPO refers to health care providers that are part of a managed care network of hospitals, clinics, doctors, and pharmacies. They participate at a reduced rate, which means the health care system provides discounts to subscribers for their services.

Medicare Advantage & HMOs

Medicare Advantage and Health Maintenance Organizations (HMOs) are various private health insurance policies underwritten by private carriers. An insurance professional can provide a side-by-side comparison of plans to determine if dental, vision, and hearing benefits are options that fit your health and lifestyle. There are variations of Medicare Advantage Plans; some are HMOs, which are health maintenance organizations. This means you have to work within their provider network. If you go outside that network, you pay for it 100% out of pocket. As not all possible HMO’s (Medicare Advantage) are available in every zip code, you must work with an accomplished Medicare Insurance professional.

Medicare Supplements ("Med Supps")

Medicare Supplements, also known as Med Sups, Medigap, or MedSupp insurance plans, will cover expenses that Original Medicare Part A and Part B do not. Medicare supplements offer you the essential element of peace of mind.

PDP (PRESCRIPTION DRUG PLANS)

A Prescription Drug (PDP) plan helps lower the costs of medications. Each plan has different formulary’s or drug lists, coverage levels for brand name and generic prescription drugs, and tiered co-pays. Your dedicated insurance agent will find the right one to meet your needs.
