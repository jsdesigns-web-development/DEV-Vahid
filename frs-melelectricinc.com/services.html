Residential

Installation.

We can wire your new construction home or remodel project. We can also provide a panel upgrade, add lighting fixtures or just install a fan.

If you are installing solar panels, a pool or spa, or purchasing an electric vehicle, we can provide the hook up you will need to power your new addition.

Service and Maintenance.

You want everything in your home to get proper power. This can be compromised by any part of the electrical system — be it wiring, outlet, electrical panel or anything else.

At MEL, we can diagnose the issue and resolve it, while giving you honest advice about all your options.
