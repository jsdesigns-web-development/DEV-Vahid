Meet MEL Electric Inc

MEL Electric Inc was started by Matt Lopez as part of his natural progression forward in the industry and his desire to provide the best customer service and quality. To provide its clients with undivided attention, the company has grown to six employees, including five crew members who are out in the field every day. The staff has over 40 years of combined electrical experience. Each crew member receives additional training by Matt with a focus on customer service and quality work, as well as continuing education courses that include code changes.

You have choices when it comes to hiring an electrician Save time and start with us today.

Matt Lopez

- Owner/Electrician

Matt Lopez is a licensed Napa electrician that has been in the field for over 15 years, involved in just about every aspect of the electrical world. He is also great at finding and fixing existing electrical problems. His seven-year formal training covered all aspects of the commercial and residential electrical industry. He graduated from the International Brotherhood of Electrical Workers Joint Apprenticeship Training Committee in Napa. Matt is very easy to work with because of his respectful nature and great attitude. He takes the time to listen to his clients to understand what will work best for them. He treats every client like they are the only one that matters. While trying to save the client money where he can, Matt always puts customer service and quality before anything else.
