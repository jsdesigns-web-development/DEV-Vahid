About Us

About the agency.

Accredited by The Joint Commission and a proud member of the Better Business Bureau is locally owned and has been in business since 1997. We are an on-call nursing agency that has a team of staffing coordinators that will assist your family, facility, or hospital with your staffing needs. Relief Nursing Services, Inc. provides reliable and dependable Registered Nurses, Licensed Vocational Nurses, Certified Nursing Assistants, Caregivers, Homemakers, and Companions to meet the challenging needs of the client and their families.

24 Hours a day, 7 days a week.

Relief Nursing Services, Inc. is the only agency in the San Francisco Bay Area affiliated with Providence Vocational School, a school that trains Caregivers to become Certified Nursing Assistants, and where our nurses go for skills enhancement and training and state certification.
